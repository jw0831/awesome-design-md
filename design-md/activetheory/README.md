# Active Theory

> Creative Digital Experiences — WebGL 3D agency portfolio

## About

[Active Theory](https://activetheory.net/) is a creative digital agency founded in 2012 that blends story, art & technology. Their website is a full-screen WebGL 3D experience built on a custom engine (Hydra), featuring metallic 3D objects, particle systems, and scroll-driven camera movement through 3D scenes.

## Design Characteristics

- **Pure void black** background with WebGL-rendered 3D content
- **NBArchitektStd** — monospaced architect-blueprint typeface, all uppercase
- **Frosted glass** UI overlays with `backdrop-filter: blur`
- **Violet accent** (`#b8a0d4`) for terminal-style category navigation
- **3D project card carousel** in perspective depth
- **Zero traditional layout** — no grids, no cards, no shadows

## Font Substitution

NBArchitektStd is a commercial typeface by Neubau Berlin. For implementations, use:
- **IBM Plex Mono** (closest match — monospaced, geometric)
- **Space Mono** (alternative — monospaced, editorial)
- **JetBrains Mono** (alternative — monospaced, technical)
